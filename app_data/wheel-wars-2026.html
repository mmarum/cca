Wheel Wars 5! 2026

Special 5th Anniversary Edition!

Sat Mar 21, 2026 , 2-5PM

Competition will take place @ Division Brewing, 508 E Main St, Arlington, TX 76010

FREE COMMUNITY EVENT

Wheel Wars is a fast and fun pottery wheel competition. This year, to celebrate the 5th anniversary, four local DFW pottery studios will have teams competing against each other on 4 wheels. Challenges will be tougher and more chaotic than before. Points from the Judge will be accumulated from each challenge to determine the winning studio.

CHALLENGES MAY INCLUDE:

One handed, tallest form, most bowls, teapot relay and "ghost" challenge.
