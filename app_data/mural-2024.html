Call for muralist

Catalyst Creative Arts is partnering with our Sponsor, the Arlington Museum of Art to provide a community mural art opportunity for a local DFW artist.

Mural details:

8 ft x 12 ft mural that will be displayed fulltime on the exterior of a historic Downtown Arlington building in the heart of the Cultural Arts District; near Theater Arlington, City Hall, Arlington Public Library, and the Levitt Pavilion. Location is 300 W. Main St., Arlington, Texas.

When:

The selected mural will remain on the mural space until December 31, 2024. The mural space will display 2 different artist's work per year, for 6 months each. The next mural selection process will begin in late 2024.

Selection:

Artist applicants will be selected by a panel of community members.

Dates:
Application submissions will close on March 25, 2024. Selection will be announced March 29, 2024.The winning artist will complete the mural by April 21, 2024.

The muralist will receive $1836 upon completion of the project. ($1536 for work & $300 for material)

Applicants must complete the sign up form on this page and submit via email a sketch or mockup of a mural for this project, along with 1-3 photo examples of previous mural work to:

info@catalystcreativearts.com
Please use your last name as the email SUBJECT line.
