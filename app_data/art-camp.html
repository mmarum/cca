Catalyst Creative Arts Summer Art Camp is a great place for your child to learn new art skills and techniques. Each day, at camp, we will be working on projects that challenge our students to learn and practice new techniques to build upon their art foundations. Projects are different each day and will include 2 pottery projects, paintings, 3D construction projects, printing, watercolor and a self portrait.
