Camp:

Monday-Friday, 9am-1pm (drop off as early as 8:30am and pick up as late as 1:30pm)

Water and a light snack provided daily. Students may also bring their own.

Pottery Camp Description:

Catalyst Creative Arts Summer POTTERY Camp is a great place for your child to learn new art skills and techniques. For 2026 we are offering 2 camps that will focus completely on pottery. Each day students will spend half of their time learning and working on handbuilding projects and half of their time on pottery wheel projects. Pottery camp projects include, lidded jar, serving plate, mugs, bowls, cups and vases. Each student will schedule and return to the studio for a single day of glazing as early as two weeks after camp ends during one of the studio's regular glazing sessions.

Art Camp Description:

Catalyst Creative Arts Summer ART Camp is a great place for your child to learn new art skills and techniques. Each day at camp, we will be working on projects that challenge our students to learn and practice new techniques to build upon their art foundations. 2026 projects are different each day and will include fused glass, printmaking, acrylic painting, a 3D construction project, watercolor, oil pastel and a self portrait or friend portrait.

Campers:

  • - can bring their own water bottles and snacks, but water and light snacks are also provided daily
  • - can bring a phone, but they are not to be used during camp
  • - must have fingernails trimmed no longer than the end of the finger tip (for pottery projects)
  • - may be given assigned seating by instructors
