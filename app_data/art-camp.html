Art Camp 2023

2023 Summer Art Camp at Catalyst Creative Arts will feature our classic favorites like pottery wheel and water marbling and will include several new projects. We will be at our NEW LOCATION starting February 1, 2023. 300 W Main St, Arlington, TX 76010.

$200 per camper. $180 per additional sibling.

Camp is from 9am to 12pm. Drop off begins at 8:30am and pick up ends at 12:30pm.

No refunds after 1 week prior to each camp start date.
