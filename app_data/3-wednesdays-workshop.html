Beginner Printmaking Workshop

Wednesdays, 3/31 - 4/14
Wed Mar 30, 2022 at 6:30 PM (Week 1)
Wed Apr 6, 2022 at 6:30 PM (Week 2)
Wed Apr 13, 2022 at 6:30 PM (Week 3)

Join us for this 3 Wednesdays (3 part) Printmaking Workshop. Workshop takes place on 3 consecutive Wednesdays, March 30 to April 6, from 6:30 to 9:00 pm. You will learn the basics of relief block printing including design layout, cutting a 9 x 12 inch linoleum block, print registration and using a print press. This is a 3 session, artist guided workshop. All material will be supplied including linoleum carving tools. Class is limited to 8 students. Workshop cost is $115.
