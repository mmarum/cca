3 Wednesdays Workshops

Beginner Printmaking Workshop

Wednesdays, 3/31 - 4/14

Join us for this 3 Wednesdays Workshop. You will learn the basics of relief block printing including design layout, cutting a 9 x 12 inch linoleum block, print registration and using a print press. This is a 3 session, artist guided workshop. All material included. Class is limited to 10 students. Workshop cost is $115.

Ceramic Succulent Garden

Wednesdays, 4/28 - 5/12

Join us for this 3 Wednesdays Workshop. You will first choose from two types of clay and then use a variety of hand building techniques (we will teach you) to build a succulent garden planter. Then you will glaze(paint) the planter. Finally you will customize and plant a succulent garden into the planter you made. This is a 3 session, artist guided workshop. All material is included. Class is limited to 10 students. Workshop cost is $100.

Vintage Racecar Art Deco Style Painting

Wednesdays, 6/9 - 6/23

Join us for this 3 Wednesdays Workshop. You will choose a great racing photo of your favorite vehicle from racing history and email the photo to us. The photo will be used to stencil, inspire, design and acrylic paint a vintage art deco style racing poster on a 36 x 48 inch canvas. This is a 3 session workshop. All material included. Class is limited to 8 people. Workshop cost is $115.

Abstract Painting

Wednesdays, 7/21 - 8/4

Join us for this 3 Wednesdays Workshop. During this 3 session workshop, you will work closely with our artist to review and sample abstract painting technique in order to design, develop and acrylic paint your abstract painting on a 30 x 40 inch or 36 x 36 inch canvas. All material included. Class is limited to 8 people. Workshop cost is $115.
