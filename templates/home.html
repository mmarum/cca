Summer Camp Programs

We are bringing back our popular summer camp and extending our offering by adding 2 extra camps in order to split camps up by different ages.

This will allow each camp to be focused on activities better matched to those ages.

Wheel Wars

Wheel Wars is a fast and fun, head-to-head pottery wheel competition where eight people will compete in 3 round eliminations.

This is a FREE event and competitors are allowed to bring friends and family to watch and cheer them on.

The Arlington Experience with Jim Ross: Episode Seventeen

In the latest episode of The Arlington Experience, Jim visits with the owners of a newer business in Arlington - but also one of its most unique - Catalyst Creative Arts!

Gift Cards

Interested in sending someone a gift to come create with us? Gift certificates are available for purchase through PayPal, over the phone and in person at our studio and may be redeemed for any of our services.
