Mini Private Events

We are excited to announce a new offering of Mini Private Events. These events are for groups from 3 to 15 people. The studio will be cleaned, prepared and private to your group only. You may select from 4 time slots. Most of our activities to are designed to be 2 hours or less. You may choose a single instructor led activity or multiple DIY activities. You may schedule your event by calling the studio @ 972 446-0444. Please keep in mind some activities require 12 hours prep time. Remember, you are welcome to bring food and beverages to your event. (Time Slots: 10am, 1pm, 4pm, 7pm, daily)
